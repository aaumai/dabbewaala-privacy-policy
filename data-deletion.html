How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of your personal data from Dabbewaala, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@aumai.co.in with the subject line: "Request for Data Deletion - Dabbewaala"
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your registered email address or phone number
    • Your full name as registered in the app
    • Clear statement that you want to delete your account and all associated data
  3. Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity before processing the deletion request
  4. Processing Time: Data deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of verification

Alternative Methods to Request Data Deletion

  • In-App Request: You can also request data deletion directly from the app:
    • Go to Profile → Settings → Account Settings
    • Select "Delete Account" or "Request Data Deletion"
    • Follow the on-screen instructions
  • Customer Support: Contact our customer support team through the in-app chat feature

Types of Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving and verifying your data deletion request, we will delete the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Address Information: All saved delivery addresses
  • Order History: Complete order history and order details
  • Payment Information: Saved payment methods (note: payment transaction records may be retained for legal/compliance purposes)
  • Preferences: Food preferences, favorite kitchens, dietary restrictions
  • Communication Data: Chat messages with kitchens and delivery partners
  • Ratings and Reviews: Your ratings and reviews submitted on the platform
  • Device Information: Device IDs, push notification tokens
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and analytics data associated with your account

Types of Data That May Be Retained

Important: The following data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or business purposes, even after account deletion:

  • Financial Records: Payment transaction records, invoices, and receipts (required for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Legal Compliance: Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you personally (used for analytics and service improvement)
  • Safety and Security: Information related to fraud prevention, security incidents, or violations of terms of service
  • Completed Orders: Order information may be retained in anonymized form for business analytics, but all personal identifiers will be removed

Data Retention Period

After your account deletion request is processed:

  • Personal Data: Deleted immediately or within 30 days of verification
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax and accounting laws
  • Legal Records: Retained as required by applicable laws or until the resolution of any ongoing disputes
  • Anonymized Data: Retained indefinitely in aggregated, non-identifiable form

What Happens After Data Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • You will no longer be able to access the app with your credentials
  • Your personal information will be removed from our active databases
  • You will stop receiving marketing communications
  • Note: Some information may remain in backup systems for up to 90 days, after which it will be permanently deleted

Reactivation After Deletion

Please note that once your account and data are deleted, this action is irreversible. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Dabbewaala services again in the future.
